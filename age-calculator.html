Frequently Asked Questions

Can I calculate age for any date? ▼
Yes! Change the reference date to any past or future date to find age at that specific time.
How are months calculated? ▼
Complete calendar months only. Partial months are counted as days.
Why is my age in hours so large? ▼
A 30-year-old has lived over 262,000 hours! Time adds up fast.
