Frequently Asked Questions

Why do early payments go to interest? ▼
Interest is calculated on remaining balance. Early on, balance is highest so interest is highest. More of each payment becomes principal over time.
How much do extra payments save? ▼
$100-200 extra/month on a typical mortgage saves tens of thousands and cuts years off the loan.
What is negative amortization? ▼
When minimum payment doesn't cover interest, balance grows instead of shrinking. Avoid loans with this feature.
