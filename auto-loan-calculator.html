Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good APR for a car loan? ▼
Excellent credit (750+): 4–6%. Good credit (700+): 6–9%. Fair credit: 9–14%. Always compare multiple lenders.
Should I choose longer loan term? ▼
Longer terms lower monthly payments but increase total interest. 72-84 month loans also risk being 'underwater' (owing more than car's value).
Is 0% financing really free? ▼
0% APR is real but usually requires excellent credit and may mean giving up a cash discount. Always compare total cost both ways.
