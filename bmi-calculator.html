Frequently Asked Questions

Is BMI accurate? ▼
BMI is a useful screening tool but doesn't measure body fat directly. Athletes may show high BMI due to muscle mass.
What BMI is healthy? ▼
18.5–24.9 is considered healthy for most adults. Asian populations may have health risks at lower BMI (around 23).
How to lower BMI? ▼
Combination of healthy diet and regular exercise. Aim for 150+ minutes of moderate activity per week.
