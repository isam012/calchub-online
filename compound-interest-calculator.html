Frequently Asked Questions

What is compound interest? ▼
Interest earned on both principal AND previously accumulated interest, creating exponential growth.
What return rate is realistic? ▼
S&P 500 long-term average: ~10% nominal, ~7% after inflation. Use 6-7% for conservative planning.
What is the Rule of 72? ▼
Divide 72 by return rate to find years to double money. At 8%: 72÷8 = 9 years.
