Frequently Asked Questions

Avalanche vs Snowball method? ▼
Avalanche (highest rate first) saves most money. Snowball (smallest balance first) provides motivation. Both work — pick what keeps you consistent.
Should I pay debt or invest? ▼
If debt rate is above 6-7%, pay debt first. Below 6%, investing often wins long-term. Always pay high-interest debt (10%+) before investing.
How to find extra money for debt? ▼
Cut subscriptions, meal prep, sell unused items, pick up gig work. Even $50-100 extra/month makes huge difference over time.
