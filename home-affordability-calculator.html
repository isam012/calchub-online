Home Affordability Calculator

How to Use

Enter income, debts, down payment, and rate to find your maximum home price using the 28/36 debt-to-income rule.

💡 28/36 Rule: Housing ≤28% of gross income. Total debt ≤36% of gross income.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 28/36 rule? ▼
Housing costs (PITI) should not exceed 28% of gross income. Total debt payments should not exceed 36%.
How much down payment do I need? ▼
Minimum 3-5% for conventional, 3.5% for FHA. 20% avoids PMI and saves hundreds per month.
What affects how much I can borrow? ▼
Credit score, debt-to-income ratio, down payment, employment history, and current interest rates all affect loan amount.
