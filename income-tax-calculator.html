Frequently Asked Questions

What is effective vs marginal tax rate? ▼
Marginal = your highest bracket rate. Effective = total tax ÷ total income. Only income within each bracket is taxed at that rate.
What is the standard deduction 2024? ▼
Single: $14,600. Married Filing Jointly: $29,200. Head of Household: $21,900.
How to reduce tax bill? ▼
Maximize 401k/IRA contributions, HSA, itemize if above standard deduction, harvest tax losses, and claim all eligible credits.
