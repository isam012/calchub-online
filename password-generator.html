How to Use the Password Generator

Choose length, count, and character types. Click Generate. All passwords are created in your browser and never sent anywhere.

Security Tips

  • Use a unique password for every account
  • Use a password manager (Bitwarden is free)
  • Enable 2FA everywhere possible
  • Never share passwords

Frequently Asked Questions

Are passwords stored? ▼
No. Everything happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Works completely offline.
How long should passwords be? ▼
Basic accounts: 12+. Important accounts: 16+. Use all character types for maximum security.
What is the best password manager? ▼
Bitwarden (free), 1Password, or Dashlane. Never reuse passwords across sites.
