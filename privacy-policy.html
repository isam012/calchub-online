📋 Short version: CalcHub does not collect your personal data or financial inputs. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We use Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics and will use Google AdSense for ads. Your numbers never leave your device.

About Sana

CalcHub is a product of Sana — a technology company building useful digital products for people worldwide. Sana operates multiple digital products and platforms, with CalcHub being its financial tools division. Sana is committed to user privacy, transparent data practices, and building products that genuinely help people make better financial decisions.

1. Who We Are

CalcHub is operated by Sana, a technology company. Our website is located at calchub-online-gamma.vercel.app. We provide free financial calculators for mortgage, loans, investments, taxes, and more.

When this Privacy Policy refers to "we," "us," "our," or "Sana," it refers to Sana and its CalcHub product. When it refers to "you," it means you, the user of CalcHub.

2. What Data We Collect

We take a privacy-first approach. Here is exactly what we do and do not collect:

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identification
  • Your financial data (loan amounts, salaries, investments)
  • Your calculator inputs — they stay on your device only
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Location data beyond country/region level

We automatically collect (anonymous only):

  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics)
  • Browser type, device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Country/region of visit (not precise location)
  • How you arrived at our site (search, direct, referral)

3. How We Use Data

The anonymous usage data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which calculators people find most useful
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Understand geographic demand to build better features

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand anonymous usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data like pages visited, session duration, and device type. This data is aggregated and anonymous — it cannot identify you personally.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

CalcHub uses (or will use) Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads based on your browsing history across websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google's Ad Settings. Non-personalized ads will still appear.

For full details on how Google uses data for advertising, visit Google's Advertising Policies.

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. CalcHub uses cookies for:

  • Preferences: Remembering your currency selection across visits
  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies for usage statistics
  • Advertising: Google AdSense cookies for relevant ad serving

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect currency preferences but calculators will still work fully.

7. Third-Party Services

CalcHub uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — advertising (Ad Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography rendering (font files)
  • Vercel — website hosting infrastructure

8. Calculator Data Security

All calculator computations — including loan amounts, salaries, investment values, tax figures — are processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No financial data is transmitted to our servers at any point. Your inputs are never stored, logged, or accessible to us.

9. Children's Privacy

CalcHub is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. International Users

CalcHub is a global product serving users worldwide. By using CalcHub, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. Since we do not collect personal financial data, cross-border data transfer concerns are minimal.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right to know what data is collected about you
  • Right to opt out of analytics (Google Analytics Opt-out)
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • GDPR rights (EU users): access, rectification, erasure, portability
  • CCPA rights (California users): know, delete, opt-out of sale

Since we collect only anonymous analytics data, most individual data rights do not apply to CalcHub specifically.
