Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2025

📋 Short version: We don't collect personal data. All calculations happen in your browser. We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense which may use cookies.

1. Who We Are

CalcHub ("we", "our", "us") operates the website calchub-online.netlify.app (the "Service"). We provide free online calculators for finance, health, math, and other everyday uses.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or financial details.

All calculator inputs you enter are processed entirely within your browser and are never sent to our servers.

We may automatically collect non-personal technical data including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data using cookies.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time spent on each page
  • Your approximate location (country/city)
  • Device and browser information

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's advertising policies.

5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to track usage patterns
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant ads
  • Preferences: Saving your language preference on our site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics – website usage analytics
  • Google AdSense – advertising
  • Google Fonts – typography (font files loaded from Google servers)
  • Netlify – website hosting

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure. All calculator computations happen locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most data rights do not apply. However, you have the right to:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Clear cookies from your browser at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
