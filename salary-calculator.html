Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert annual to hourly? ▼
Annual ÷ (hours/day × days/week × 52 weeks). Example: $60,000 ÷ 2,080 = $28.85/hour.
What is biweekly pay? ▼
Paid every two weeks — 26 times per year. Most common US pay frequency.
How to compare job offers? ▼
Convert all offers to the same unit (annual or hourly) for fair comparison.
